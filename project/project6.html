Chinese Food

Chinese has the history of thousands of years, during the development of economic, Chinese people also develop different kinds of food among the country

First of all, the custom reason, what people eat in certain geography region is because of local products and customs, for example, there are more cattle and sheep in the North part of China, so people may cook with beef and mutton. In southern China, there are more aquatic products and poultry, so people like eat fish, chicken and duck. For the coastal regions, there are more seafood dishes on the table.

Secondly, there are also climate reasons. Different climates can form different tastes, generally speaking, in north China people are more likely to cook their food thick and salty. For the east part of China, for example in Shanghai, there are sweet and sour ribs, the climate is mild, dishes are more likely to be sweet and salty. The south part of China, where the weather is rainy and humid, dishes are often spicy.

Thirdly, different cooking method, Cooking methods vary from place to place, which forms different characteristics of dishes. For example, in Shandong province, which is located in the north part of China cuisine are good at fried, roasted. There are Four-Joy meatballs, which is served with fried meatballs. In Sichuan province, which is located in the south part of China, where people cook their food by methods of stir-frying. Sichuan province is humidity and people like to eat chili to protect themselves from the weather. I think Stir-Fry is the best way to show the real flavor of hot chili.
